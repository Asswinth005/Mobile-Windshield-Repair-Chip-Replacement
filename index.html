PROFESSIONAL GUIDE

Repair vs Replace – Know What's Best

Chips smaller than a quarter can usually be repaired, but cracks longer than 6 inches may need full replacement.

✅ REPAIR

Chip Repair – Quick & Economical

  • Best for bullseyes, star breaks & combo chips < 1 inch
  • Prevents crack propagation, restores structural integrity
  • Takes 30 minutes – no disassembly
  • Usually covered fully by comprehensive insurance
💰 Typical Cost: $80 – $150 | Time: 30-45min
⚠️ REPLACE

Full Windshield Replacement

  • Long cracks > 6 inches, edge cracks, deep damage
  • Compromised driver's vision area (size of a dollar bill)
  • Advanced driver assistance recalibration (ADAS)
  • OEM-quality glass + 5-year warranty
💰 Typical Cost: $350 – $950 (insurance deductible applies)
