Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

Do you come to my location?

Yes! We are a fully mobile service. Our technicians come to your home, office, or anywhere you are. We serve the entire Phoenix metro area.

How long does a chip repair take?

Most chip repairs take 20-30 minutes. Full windshield replacements take approximately 60-90 minutes, plus 1 hour for adhesive to cure before driving.

Do you work with insurance companies?

Absolutely! We work with all major insurance providers and handle the claims process for you. Many policies cover chip repairs with $0 deductible.

What is your warranty?

We offer a lifetime warranty on all chip repairs and a 5-year warranty on windshield replacements. Our warranty is transferable if you sell your vehicle.

How do I track my technician?

Once your appointment is confirmed, you'll receive a link to our live tracking dashboard where you can see your technician's ETA in real-time.
