Frequently Asked Questions

Is the warranty transferable if I sell my car?

Yes! Our warranty is fully transferable to the next owner. Simply provide them with the original service receipt and warranty card.

How do I file a warranty claim?

Call our warranty department at (888) 450-SHLD or email claims@shieldmate.com with your claim ID and photos of the issue. We'll schedule a free inspection within 48 hours.

Does the warranty cover cracks that develop after repair?

Yes. If your chip repair fails and a crack develops from the original damage site, we'll reimburse the repair cost or apply it toward a full replacement.

Where is the warranty valid?

Our warranty is valid nationwide across all states where ShieldMate operates. If you move, your coverage moves with you.

Do I need to pay anything for warranty service?

No. Warranty-covered repairs and inspections are completely free of charge. There are no hidden fees or deductibles.
