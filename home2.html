Repair vs Replace

We help you make the right decision for safety and savings

Choose Repair When

  • ✓ Chip smaller than a quarter
  • ✓ Not in driver's line of sight
  • ✓ No deep cracks spreading
  • ✓ Save money ($0 deductible often)
Best Value

Choose Replace When

  • ✓ Crack longer than 6 inches
  • ✓ Damage at edge of glass
  • ✓ Deep multiple fractures
  • ✓ ADAS recalibration needed
